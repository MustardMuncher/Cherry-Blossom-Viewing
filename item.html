Item

Some great snacks to bring to a cherry blossom viewing include sakura mochi, sakura dango, sakura tea, and sakura-flavored treats.

Sweet treats

Sakura mochi: Chewy rice cakes with sweet bean paste and wrapped in cherry blossom leaves

Hanami dango: Rice balls that come in pink, white, and green

Sakura manju: Steamed buns with red bean and sakura leaves

Sakura yokan: A jelly made of agar-agar that's vegan

Sakura KitKat: A limited-edition chocolate bar

Sakura Pocky: A limited-edition snack

Savory snacks

Sakura onigiri: Pickled cherry blossoms stuffed into rice and wrapped in a pickled sakura leaf

Sakura prawn crackers: A cherry blossom-flavored snack from Calbee

Drinks

Sakura tea: Pairs well with sakura mochi

Sakura sake and beer: Limited-edition drinks

Other treats

Sakura jelly, which showcases cherry blossoms inside light pink jelly

Sakura-themed cakes, donuts, cookies, and mont blancs

Sakura inarizushi
